About Us

Welcome to Unbounded Research and Development, a cutting-edge computer graphics company led by Dr. Russell Campbell. As a trusted subcontractor for larger studios, we specialize in developing and enhancing graphics systems for video games and film. Our team of experts is committed to pushing the boundaries of computer graphics and delivering innovative solutions that elevate the visual quality and performance of our clients' projects.

Our founders, who have been esteemed speakers at the Game Developers Conference (GDC) Technical Artist Summit, are renowned in the industry for their expertise in computer graphics. We are proud to be actively involved in groundbreaking research that advances the field, constantly exploring new techniques, algorithms, and tools to stay at the forefront of the industry.

In addition to our research and development work, we also offer cutting-edge asset packs for purchase, including highly sought-after shaders. Our meticulously crafted assets are designed to be seamlessly integrated into existing pipelines, saving time and effort for artists and developers alike.

At Unbounded, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, innovation, and collaboration. Our passion for computer graphics drives us to deliver outstanding results for our clients and contribute to their success. Whether you're a studio looking for a trusted partner to enhance your graphics systems or an artist seeking high-quality assets, you can rely on Unbounded's expertise and dedication to bring your graphics vision to life. Contact us today to learn more about how we can collaborate to create unparalleled visual experiences.

So, join us on this unbounded journey of pushing the limits of computer graphics and unlocking new possibilities for the entertainment industry. Thank you for considering Unbounded Research and Development as your partner in computer graphics innovation. You're only limited by your imagination, and with Unbounded, the possibilities are endless. Join us today!
